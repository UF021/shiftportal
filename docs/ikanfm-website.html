Our Story
Who We Are

Ikan Facilities Management specialises in providing manned security and physical risk management services primarily to the cinema industry. With over two decades of industry experience and clients in England, Scotland, and Wales, we are the single largest provider of manned security to the cinema industry.

We know what our clients want and we aim to deliver an effective, efficient, and reliable service. Our teams are key to our service delivery and we focus on having a streamlined management structure to improve efficiency and deliver a more agile service.

🎯

Sector Focused

We don't spread ourselves thin across every industry. Our expertise is the cinema sector — and that depth of specialisation shows in our service quality.

⚡

Streamlined Structure

A lean management layer means decisions are made fast, rosters are filled quickly, and issues are resolved without layers of bureaucracy.

🤝

Long-Term Partnerships

Our client relationships span years, not months. We invest in understanding each venue and building relationships with the teams that run them.

Requirements

What we look for

We welcome applications from individuals who take pride in their work and are committed to delivering excellent customer-facing security services.

  • ✓ Valid SIA Door Supervisor or Security Guard licence (or willingness to obtain one)
  • ✓ Right to work in the United Kingdom
  • ✓ Professional appearance and strong communication skills

  • ✓ Ability to work evenings and weekends (cinema hours)
